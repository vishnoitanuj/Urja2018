About us

Who We Are

Whether it be an aggressive one as football or a passive one like chess, life is the ultimate sport. It takes focus and determination to win this ultimate game. As we get to pick the game and the rules, we dare the universe to bring us the challenge, thus, gives us the power to rise above all and achieve the impossible. Life is a sport. It’s the de facto game. From the day we are born, we spend the first 18 years as a tutorial on what we will do next. As we grow up we gain experience in any field and suddenly, we get dragged into hectic timetables, we often neglect about our well-being. And work becomes something around just getting paid. In other words, the object becomes to make money, go home and spend it. But a few of them only pay attention to physical and mental health. Here at URJA’ 18, we will make you focus on the Game in the Game. By focusing on one game at a time, we wish to help you regain what’s been lost and also to relinquish your thirst for competitive success and victory. We encourage people to Come Out and Play. Leave that introvert at home and come be a part of something great. Something bigger than life itself. Because here at URJA’18, we will be there to provide you a platform so you can shine a little brighter. One Game at a Time, i.e. Sports.

What We Do

URJA is an endeavor to provide a national platform to the youth to showcase their talents and skill sets in fierce competitions. It will enhance sportsmanship amongst youth. We plan to organize the following sports in URJA’18:

  • Athletics - 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, 4×100m
  • Shot-Put (Men & Women)
  • Discus Throw (Men)
  • Badminton – Men & Women
  • Basketball – Men & Women
  • Chess – Men & Women
  • Football – Men
  • Lawn Tennis – Men & Women
  • Table Tennis – Men & Women
  • Volleyball – Men
  • Cricket – Men
