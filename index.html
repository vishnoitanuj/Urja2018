Cricket

Cricket is a game which owes much of it’s unique appeal to the fact that it is played not only within it’s laws but also within spirit of the game.
Historically game of cricket is symbolised by etiquette , manner and fairplay.Cricket is a team game and each and every player is expected to make a contribution in a positive way.
It is a game in which players as well as coaches are bestowed with the responsibility of ensuring that the game is played with the right spirit.

Badminton

Badminton is the Fastest Racket Sport with shuttle clocking speed in excess of 200mph. It isthe second most popular sport in the world, after soccer. It is a very fitness demanding sport.There are a great deal of attributes a person needs in order to become successful. The mainfitness components are agility, speed, muscular endurance, muscular power andcardio respiratory endurance.

Athletics

Athletics is a term encompassing the human competitive sports and games requiring physicalskill, and the systems of training that prepare athletes for competition performance. Athleticsports or contests, are competitions which are primarily based on human, physical competition,demanding the qualities of stamina, fitness, and skill. Athletic sports form the bulk of popularsporting activities, with other major forms including motorsports, precision sports, extremesports and animal sports.

Football

Football arguably is the most followed sport in the world. But what sets the “beautiful game” apart from others is the sheer charm which lies in its simplicity. Simple by the rules but intricate in emotions, it's not just a leisure for the ones who love the game, the feeling runs much deeper than that. Every time you enter the pitch, you leave all the thoughts behind to enter a surreal world rendered by your passion, hard-work, and love for the game. As Cristiano Ronaldo once quoted: “I’m living a dream I never want to wake up from”

Chess

“Checkmate.” The word holds the divine authority; the game is over. Chess is an enthralling clash of wits that can prolong hours for a single game. Every move is critical, and a slip of concentration even for a moment can spell the end. Whether triumph or failure, you know it is entirely because of how you played during the game. You cannot pawn off your failures on others, and nobody can claim credit for your success.

Lawn Tennis

Tennis is believed to have originated in the monastic cloisters in northern France in the 12thcentury. It is an Olympic sport, and it can be played by wheelchair users as well.It is also played by millions of recreational players and is also a popular worldwide spectatorsport. It is basically is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles).
