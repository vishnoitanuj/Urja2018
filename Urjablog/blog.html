Welcome to the Official blog of Urja 2018

If there's one thing we can say for sure about 2017, its the fact that India did exceedingly well in terms of sporting achievements. Indian teams and individuals captured imaginations and redefined boundaries set by their predecessors.
1. PV Sindhu faltering at the final step, struck silver in the BWF Super Series finals
2. Indian women's hockey team equaled the achievement of the male counterparts by winning the Asian Hockey Cup
3. Pankaj Advani rediscovered his best self at Asian Team Snooker Championship
4. India's hitman completes his hat-trick of Double Centuries
5. Rohan Bopanna struck gold at the French Open in the Mixed Doubles category
6. India fulfilled their dream of a FIFA event by hosting the U-17 World Cup
7.Indian Women's team finished as Runners-Up at the ICC World Cup.
Looking back at all of these, achievement and laurels accumulated by Indian sportsmen and athletes can't be overstated.
Here's to hoping they continue their great run in the year to come!
